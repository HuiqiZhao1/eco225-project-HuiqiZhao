LSC Publishes 2015 Notice of Intent to Award

2015 Grant Assurances now available; Certification due Aug. 18

The 2015 Grant Assurances and the 2015 Certification Form are now available from the Reference Materials page. All grant applicants must review and sign both documents, and return the Certification Form using the online application system by August 18, 2014.
